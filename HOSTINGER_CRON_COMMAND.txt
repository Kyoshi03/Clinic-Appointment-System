HOSTINGER CRON JOB FOR GLOBALIFE APPOINTMENT REMINDERS
=====================================================

Use this in Hostinger > Websites > globalife.online > Advanced > Cron Jobs.

Schedule:
*/10 * * * *

Command:
/usr/bin/php /home/u901773288/domains/globalife.online/public_html/cron/send_appointment_reminders.php

What it does:
- checks confirmed appointments that are near their visit time
- sends the patient reminder by email
- sends the patient reminder by SMS
- does not resend email again if only SMS failed
- retries failed reminder delivery up to the system limit

Important:
Do not open cron/send_appointment_reminders.php in the browser. It is made for Cron Jobs only.

If Hostinger says the path is invalid:
1. Open Hostinger File Manager.
2. Go to domains/globalife.online/public_html.
3. Copy the full server path.
4. Replace /home/u901773288/domains/globalife.online/public_html with that exact path.
